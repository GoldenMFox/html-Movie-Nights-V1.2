Genre: Sci-Fi / Action / Drama

Michael Morbius, a brilliant scientist battling a rare blood disease, turns to experimental vampire bat DNA for a cure. Success grants him extraordinary powers, yet a dire side effect emerges: he becomes a living vampire.

Initially a heroic figure, Morbius battles his insatiable bloodlust, teetering between savior and menace. His nemesis, Milo, also a vampire, wields greater power and a sinister agenda.

In this dark, gripping superhero tale, Morbius grapples with the thin line between good and evil, driven by science, and haunted by temptation.
