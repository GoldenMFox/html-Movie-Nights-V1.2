Overview

Oppenheimer is a biographical drama film about J. Robert Oppenheimer, the scientist who led the Manhattan Project to develop the atomic bomb during World War II. The film explores the moral and ethical dilemmas that Oppenheimer faced as he witnessed the destruction caused by his creation. It is a story of both triumph and tragedy, as Oppenheimer grapples with the immense power he has unleashed into the world.
